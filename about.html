About Us

Our Goals

Kitties Galore goal is to find the perfect home for all our lovely cats. There a great many cats who don’t have homes and are in need of loving families to take them in to show them love and kindness they deserve.


Who We Are

We are a group of people with a vested interest in seeing all of our cats getting adopted by into the right homes for both the cats’ and the loving families who adopted.
